About us

Established in April 2022, with the objective of making and distributing concrete products as well as other building materials regionally and nationally in Kenya with specific activity in the supply of concrete blocks, Quarry dust, sand and stones as well as transport services and construction machinery hire.

The building materials supplied include;

  1. Blocks
  2. Sand
  3. Ballast
  4. Quary dust
  5. Building stones
  6. Etc
  1. Machinery plant and office on Lurambi-Sichilai- New Sheywe Market Road (Near SDA Church) Kakamega Town.
  2. Distribution Point-Lwandeti Market through the Telemus Cyber and Electronics, Lwandeti-Lugari Market Junction.
